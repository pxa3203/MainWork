About Me:

First and foremost, I live and breathe tech. Ever since I was young my mind has been fascinated with all shapes and forms of technology from hardware to software and anything in between. I remember writing my first “Hello World” print line statement in Java and being amazed that it was something that I, myself created. It led me down the rabbit hole that is Information Technology and introduced me to complex issues solved by technology. I firmly believe that the power of technology can improve the lives of people around the world. It is a never-ending puzzle filled with challenges and concepts new to me every day and that is why I love it! This mindset has taught me problem-solving and critical thinking skills that one cannot learn from the classroom.

My programming education is in the following technologies:
• Java
• Python
• HTML5
• PHP
• CSS3
• JavaScript
• Boostrap
• JQuery
• SQL

When I am not working on assignments or completing tasks directly correlated to IT, I find myself wondering how technologies we use in our everyday lives work. From massive screens in Times Square to my credit card’s contactless payment feature, I am always curious and inquiring on how technologies operate and how they could possibly be improved.
